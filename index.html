Our Story

Kelly's Country Smokehouse is a true East Texas smokehouse located off the beaten path near Lake Murvaul. What started as Chuck's Country Smoke House has been reimagined with the same love for great BBQ and community.

Owned and operated by Luke, the focus remains on quality meats, homemade sides, and that small-town hospitality. We also cater private events and special occasions — just give us a call.

The Food

Slow-smoked the right way. From tender brisket and massive dino ribs to house-made sausages and creative specials, everything is made with care.
